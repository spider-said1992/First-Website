Here's a time line of Abraham Lincoln's life:


  • 1806-Lincoln is born near Hodgenvile,Kentucky, United States
  • 1834-Lincoln is elected to the Illinois state legislature.
  • 1847-Lincoln enters the U.S. House of Representatives.
  • 1858-Lincoln wins national fame in debates with Stephen A.Douglas.
  • 1860-Lincoln is elected president.
  • 1861-The civil War begins.
  • Jan.1.1863-Lincoln issues the Emancipation proclamation.
  • Nov.19.1863-Lincoln gives the Gettysburg Adress.
  • April 9.1865-The Civil War ends.
  • April 14.1865-Lincoln is shot;he dies the next morning.
